1928 Cadillac Town Sedan

The custom-made 1928 Cadillac Town Sedan was built for America's most notorious gangster Al Capone. The massive vehicle was bulletproof with 1-inch thick glass windows and steel-plate armor on side doors. It also had a police siren mounted under the hood and a police radio in the glove compartment.
